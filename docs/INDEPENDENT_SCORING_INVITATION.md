was not handed — the Einstein Test (could an AI, given only what was known in
1911, derive general relativity by 1915?), and now non-physics probes (Darwin
pre-1858, Mendel pre-1865, the marginal revolution pre-1871) where the
contamination floor is lower.

https://pathtoagi-observatory.netlify.app/experiments/fcs-1-2026-07-01/

suite's own asymmetry, a self-graded pass is an upper bound and nothing more.

**The ask:** would you serve as an independent scorer for one or more FCS
runs? Concretely:

- You receive the frozen artifacts (prompt, transcripts, rubric) — the same

- You grade against the pre-registered rubric, in whatever depth you judge

- Your grading is published verbatim on the record, under your name, alongside
  the instrument's own — including wherever you disagree with it.
- No compensation is offered, deliberately: a paid grader is a hired one. The
  time cost is roughly one to three hours per run.

The record's honest state, including its retracted errors, is at
https://pathtoagi-observatory.netlify.app/log/ — I would rather you judge the
instrument by its mistakes than by this letter.
