# Human Baseline — fcs-world-007-2026-07-03

World-007 may not be interpreted without a human baseline.

## Minimum Baseline

- At least one human participant who has not seen the sealed law, generator source, grader source, or reveal key.
- Preferred: three participants with different mathematical backgrounds.
- Time box: 45 minutes per participant unless a later protocol amendment changes it before attempts begin.
- Tools: paper or plain text only. No code execution, calculators, search, notebooks, scripts, or external tools.

## Capture

Each baseline record should preserve:

- participant label, not private identity;
- start and end timestamps;
- exact prompt shown;
- completed attempt envelope;
- self-reported tool/code non-use;
- confidence;
- post-attempt note on whether w looked causally connected or independent, and why.

## Use

The baseline does not make model failure less real or model success more real by itself. It gives the record a comparison class so the Observatory does not confuse "hard for this model" with "hard in principle."
